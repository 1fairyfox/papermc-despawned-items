Cookies Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-21

We use no cookies

This site sets no cookies and uses no third-party tracking. It stores two things in your browser's local storage — which stays on your device, is not a cookie, and is never sent to a server: your shared Fairy Fox reader preferences (theme, accent, text size, spacing, width) and the shared Fairy Fox coins counter (your coin balance and today's opened-page record). You can clear both any time — the reader menu has a Reset control and the coins panel a small Clear my data link — or clear your browser's site data.

The plugin itself, running on a Minecraft server, sets no cookies (it has no web surface at all).
